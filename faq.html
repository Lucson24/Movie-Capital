Frequently Asked Questions

How long do I have access to my movies?

Once you have purchased a movie, you can view it as many times as you would like as long as your Movie Capital account remains active.

How do I get discounted movies?

When searching for a movie add "Yes" to the discount option, which will filter the movies and only show ones that are currently discounted.

What kind of movies are on Movie Capital?

Movie Capital has a wide variety of independent movies to choose from. Genres such as Romance, Horror, Documentaries, Superheroes, and many more can be filtered through to find exactly what you want to watch. How do I add movies to my cart?

You must be logged into Movie Capital to view and add movies to your cart. Once you are logged in and browsing movies, simply click on the movie poster you would like to order and select check out.
